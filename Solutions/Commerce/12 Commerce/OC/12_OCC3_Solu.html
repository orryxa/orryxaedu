Case Study: Soham & Navin

Mr. Soham (MBA) wants dairy farm, Mr. Navin (B.Com) wants cashier job.
i) Soham's dream: Entrepreneurship; Navin's dream: Employment. ii) Feature of Entrepreneur: Risk bearing / Innovation. iii) Qualities Soham should have: Self-confidence, determination, passion, communication, discipline.

True — generates own income, creates jobs for others, promotes independence.
Successful businessman takes calculated risk.
True — assesses probability, plans mitigation, doesn't gamble.
Entrepreneur must be a good communicator.
True — essential for dealing with customers, employees, investors.
