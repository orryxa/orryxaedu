C. State True or False with reasons

1) Inland bill is drawn in one country and payable in another.
False Inland bill is drawn and payable in the same country.
2) Retirement of bill means payment before due date.
True Retiring a bill means paying it before maturity.
3) Drawee can transfer ownership of bill.
False Only holder (drawer/endorsee) can transfer bill.
4) Acceptance without changes is called qualified acceptance.
False It is called general acceptance.
5) Discounting converts bill into present value.
True Discounting gives immediate cash less discount.
6) Bill must be presented to acceptor on due date.
True Presentment for payment is necessary.
7) If bill discounted, no entry when honoured.
True Drawer already received cash; no further entry.
8) Noting charges are borne by drawer.
False Borne by drawee (acceptor).
9) No grace days for "on demand" bill.
True Demand bills are payable immediately.
10) There are three parties to a Promissory Note.
True Maker (promisor), Payee, and endorser.

Retirement of Bill (Rebate)

Example: Nandita retired her acceptance of ₹13,550 by paying ₹13,000 cash one month before due date.
Entry in books of Drawer: Cash A/c Dr. 13,000, Rebate A/c Dr. 550 → To Bills Receivable A/c 13,550.
Entry in books of Drawee: Bills Payable A/c Dr. 13,550 → To Cash A/c 13,000, To Rebate A/c 550.
Rebate = discount given for early payment (expense for drawer, income for drawee).

Insolvency of Drawee (Acceptor)

Example: Acceptor declared insolvent, only 40% recovered from estate. Bill amount ₹20,000.
Recovery = 20,000 × 40% = ₹8,000; Bad Debts = 20,000 - 8,000 = ₹12,000.
Entry in Drawer's books: Cash A/c Dr. 8,000, Bad Debts A/c Dr. 12,000 → To Drawee's A/c 20,000.

Endorsement of Bill (Illustration)

Pallavi endorsed Vandana's acceptance of ₹22,500 to Purva. Bill dishonoured, noting charges ₹175 paid by Purva.
In Pallavi's books: Vandana's A/c Dr. 22,675 → To Purva's A/c 22,675.
In Purva's books: Pallavi's A/c Dr. 22,675 → To Bills Receivable A/c 22,500, To Cash A/c 175.
In Vandana's books: Bills Payable A/c Dr. 22,500, Noting Charges A/c Dr. 175 → To Pallavi's A/c 22,675.
