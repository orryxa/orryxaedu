📘 Chapter Summary: Computer in Accounting

🔹 Computerized Accounting System (CAS): It uses computers & IT to record, store, and process financial transactions. It generates reports as per GAAP, making decision-making quick, accurate, and timely.

📌 Features: Integrated data, Accuracy & Speed, Quick decision making, Security, Transparency, Grouping of accounts, Immediate availability of books.

🔹 Importance: Automation, Multi-user facilities, Accuracy, Speed, Reduction in cost, Systematic records, Huge storage capacity, Compact storage, Easy transferability of information.

🔹 Components: Hardware (computers, printers, network), Software (accounting programs), Personnel (people operating the system).

🔹 Accounting Software Types: Ready-to-use (small businesses), Customized (medium/large), Tailored (large multi-user), Free & Open Source (GNU Khata).

🔹 Legal vs Pirated Software: Legal software is safe, updatable, and fully functional. Pirated software is illegal, risky for data, and cannot be updated.

D. Answer in one sentence

1) What is CAS?
Computerized Accounting System (CAS) is software that records, stores, and processes financial transactions using computers and IT as per GAAP.
2) Write the steps to create Ledger account in Tally.
Gateway of Tally → Accounts Info → Ledgers → Single Ledger → Create → enter name, group, and save with Ctrl+A.
3) How to view reports in Tally?
Click on 'Display' option from Gateway of Tally or go to Reports menu and select required report (Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, etc.).
4) Explain the various types of vouchers in Tally.
F4 Contra, F5 Payment, F6 Receipt, F7 Journal, F8 Sales, F9 Purchase, and F10 for other vouchers.

Tally Voucher Types & Their Uses

Voucher TypeFunction KeyUse
Contra VoucherF4Cash deposited/withdrawn from bank, bank to bank transfer, cash to cash transfer
Payment VoucherF5All types of payments (cash and bank), credit item must be Cash or Bank A/c
Receipt VoucherF6Cash receipts and bank receipts, debit item must be Cash or Bank A/c
Journal VoucherF7Non-cash transactions like depreciation, provisions, credit purchase of fixed assets
Sales VoucherF8Cash sales and credit sales; can be "As Invoice" or "As Voucher" mode
Purchase VoucherF9Cash purchases and credit purchases; can be "As Invoice" or "As Voucher" mode

Manual Accounting vs Computerized Accounting

BasisManual AccountingComputerized Accounting
CalculationsDone manually; time-consuming, prone to errorsAutomatic calculations; error-free and fast
Ledger PreparationManual posting from journal; chances of wrong transferAutomatic posting; no transfer errors
Trial BalancePrepared manually from ledger balancesAuto-generated instantly
Adjustment EntriesRecorded and posted manually one by oneOnly entry needed; posting automatic
Financial StatementsManually prepared; time-consumingAuto-generated; updated after each entry
Closing of BooksBalances carried forward manuallyAutomatically carried forward to next year

How to Create Company in Tally (Navigation Path)

Path: Gateway of Tally → Company Info → Create Company
Details to fill: Company Name, Mailing Name, Address, Financial Year From/To, Books Starting From, Tally Vault Password, etc.
Save: Press Ctrl+A to accept.

How to Create Ledger in Tally

Path: Gateway of Tally → Accounts Info → Ledgers → Single Ledger → Create
Fill: Name of Ledger, Under (Select Group like Sundry Debtors, Sundry Creditors, etc.)
Save: Press Ctrl+A to accept.

How to View Reports in Tally

Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Select Report (Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss A/c, Day Book, etc.)
OR press shortcut keys: Alt+G (Go To) → type report name.
