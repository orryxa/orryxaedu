📖 Chapter Highlights: Trade & Its Dynamics

Wholesale Trade

Large qty, link b/w producer & retailer

Retail Trade

Direct to consumer, variety & convenience

International Trade

Import | Export | Entrepot
📌 Internal Trade: Buying & selling within country → Wholesale + Retail.
📌 Wholesaler features: Large capital, risk bearing, storage, price stability, marketing functions.
📌 Retailer types: Itinerant (Hawkers, Peddlers, Street traders, Cheap jacks, Market traders) & Fixed shop (General stores, Speciality, Second hand, Authorised dealer, Departmental store, Supermarket, Chain store, One price shop, Mall).
📌 Export Procedure: 4 stages: Preliminary → Pre-shipment → Shipment → Post-shipment.
📌 Import Procedure: Preliminary → Pre-import → Import stage → Post-import.
📌 Entrepot Trade: Import goods then re-export after processing/storage.
BasisWholesalerRetailer
CapitalLarge investmentLimited capital

⭐ Recent Trends: Online retail (Flipkart, Amazon, Myntra), cashback schemes, loyalty programs, self-service supermarkets.

Export Procedure (4 Stages)

StageKey Activities
PreliminaryRegistration (DGFT, PAN, GST), Appoint agent
Pre-shipmentOrder receipt, Letter of Credit, Pre-shipment finance, Production, Packaging, ECGC cover, GST, Marine Insurance, C&F agents
ShipmentDocument processing, Goods examination, Loading, Mate's receipt, Bill of Lading
Post-shipmentShipment advice, Document presentation to bank, Realization of incentives, Follow-up

Import Procedure (4 Stages)

StageKey Steps
PreliminaryRegistration, Negotiation with overseas supplier
Pre-importQuota certificate, Foreign exchange clearance, Order placement (Indent), Letter of Credit, Appoint C&F agent
Import stageReceipt of documents, Bill of Entry, Delivery order, Custom clearance

Justify & Case Study

Case: Sonupant buys monthly from Nandulal & bulk from Gorhe and Son's.
i) Wholesaler: Gorhe and Son's; ii) Retailer: Nandulal; iii) Difference: Wholesaler sells large qty to retailer, retailer sells small qty to consumer.
Case: Anurag sells to Japan, Kavita buys from USA, Ganesh buys from SA and sells to Malaysia.
i) Exporter: Anurag & Ganesh; ii) Importer: Kavita & Ganesh (for raw material); iii) Entrepot Trade: Ganesh (import from SA, export after processing to Malaysia)
